The Stone Skipping Randomiser

What is it?

In short: it's a page that provides you with a number of questions to answer. You get to pick the number. The page picks the questions.

Why is it useful?

Well, Havi explains it a whole lot better than me. But if you're stuck in some way, asking gentle, mindful questions and seeing what comes up is a good way to help yourself get unstuck.

How do I get my stones?

Simply enter a number and hit Enter, or click the Submit button below, and you'll be taken to a page with your random questions or stones.
