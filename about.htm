About the Stone Skipping Randomiser

About Stone Skipping

Stone Skipping is a technique for working on your patterns. The ones that live in your head.

Ask a question and write down your answer. The best stone-skipping questions are open-ended, and help you to think about your patterns in a compassionate, non-judgemental way.

If you want to find out more about Stone Skipping, check out Havi's blog.

About me

Hi! I'm Lucy McLaughlin. Among various other things, I'm a programmer, and a student of Havi.

One day, working through some stuff, and not owning any of the lovely cards Havi makes, I decided to write a small program to take some questions and randomise them.

About the Randomiser

This is that program, slightly adapted to work through a browser. The idea is simple: you simply enter the number of questions you would like to answer into the stone skipper and receive that number of random questions.
